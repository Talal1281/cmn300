Hey, I'm Talal, your TA for CMN 300! This website is made to tackle any confusion you may have. Below are a list of common questions students had about the course in previous years!

Reading quizzes are short tests made to help students understand the material better before class. They are a valuable aspect of the course as they reinforce key concepts, before in-class discussions.

Communicate with the professor and discuss what can be done. The earlier you inform them, the more likely you can find a mutual agreement.

To request an extension, contact your professor as soon as possible. Clearly and respectfully explain why you need more time and specify the duration of the extension needed. A doctor's note is not required unless specified by your professor.

With an hour and 15 minutes for 10 multiple-choice questions, you have ample time to carefully understand each question and identify key terms. Before submitting, review your answers to ensure they are clear, complete, and free from mistakes.

Step 1: Log In to D2L

Start by opening your browser and logging into D2L. Navigate to the CMN 300 course homepage.

Step 2: Locate the Assessments Tab

At the top of the course homepage, find a button called Assessments and click it to see a dropdown menu.

Step 3: Select Quizzes

Now click on Quizzes, which will direct you to the quizzes page.

Step 4: Start Your Quiz

Click the quiz you need to take, and then press the Start Quiz button.
